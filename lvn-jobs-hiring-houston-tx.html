Introduction

Are you looking for a job as a Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN) in Houston, TX? You've come to the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know about LVN jobs hiring in Houston, TX.

Qualifications

To become an LVN in Texas, you need to complete a state-approved vocational nursing program and pass the National Council Licensure Examination for Practical Nurses (NCLEX-PN). Additionally, you need to have a high school diploma or GED and be at least 18 years old.

Job Description

LVNs in Houston, TX, work in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, and home health agencies. They provide basic nursing care, such as taking vital signs, administering medications, dressing wounds, and assisting patients with daily activities.

Salary and Benefits

The average salary for LVNs in Houston, TX, is around $48,000 per year. However, the salary range can vary depending on the employer, location, and experience. Most LVN jobs in Houston, TX, offer benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, and tuition reimbursement.

Job Outlook

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the employment of LVNs in Texas is projected to grow by 9% from 2020 to 2030. The demand for LVNs in Houston, TX, is expected to increase due to the aging population and the need for long-term care services.

Top LVN Employers in Houston, TX

Here are some of the top employers that hire LVNs in Houston, TX:

Houston Methodist Hospital

Houston Methodist Hospital is a leading healthcare provider in Houston, TX, that offers various LVN job opportunities in different specialties, including pediatrics, oncology, and cardiology.

Memorial Hermann Health System

Memorial Hermann Health System is another top healthcare provider in Houston, TX, that hires LVNs for different roles, such as outpatient clinics, home health, and hospice care.

St. Joseph Medical Center

St. Joseph Medical Center is a 792-bed hospital in downtown Houston that offers LVN jobs in various departments, including surgery, critical care, and emergency.

Tips for Getting Hired

Here are some tips for getting hired as an LVN in Houston, TX:

Network

Attend job fairs, join professional associations, and connect with other LVNs in Houston, TX, to expand your network and increase your chances of getting hired.

Gain Experience

Consider volunteering or working as a nursing assistant or medical assistant to gain experience and enhance your resume.

Be Prepared for the Interview

Research the employer, practice your answers to common interview questions, and dress professionally to make a good impression.

Conclusion

LVN jobs hiring in Houston, TX, offer competitive salaries, benefits, and job growth opportunities. By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can increase your chances of getting hired and starting a rewarding career as an LVN in Houston, TX.
