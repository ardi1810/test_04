Places In Kansas City To Eat

Introduction

Kansas City, Missouri is known for its barbecue, but the city also has a diverse food scene that includes everything from farm-to-table restaurants to food trucks. If you're looking for a place to eat in Kansas City, you won't be disappointed. Here are some of the top places to check out:

1. Q39

If you're looking for some of the best barbecue in town, Q39 is the place to go. This restaurant has won numerous awards for its slow-smoked meats and homemade sauces. The atmosphere is casual, and the menu includes everything from brisket to pulled pork to burnt ends.

2. The Rieger

For a more upscale dining experience, head to The Rieger. This restaurant serves American cuisine with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, but you can always expect creative dishes and a great wine selection.

3. Joe's Kansas City Bar-B-Que

Joe's Kansas City Bar-B-Que is another must-visit for barbecue lovers. This restaurant started as a gas station and has since become a Kansas City institution. The menu includes classic barbecue dishes like ribs and brisket, as well as more unique items like burnt ends chili.

4. Novel

If you're in the mood for something a little different, check out Novel. This restaurant serves contemporary American cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients. The menu changes frequently, but you can always expect creative and flavorful dishes.

5. Port Fonda

For some of the best Mexican food in town, head to Port Fonda. This restaurant serves authentic Mexican cuisine with a modern twist. The menu includes everything from tacos to enchiladas to margaritas.

6. Westside Local

Westside Local is a farm-to-table restaurant that serves American cuisine with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, but you can always expect fresh and flavorful dishes. The restaurant also has a great selection of craft beer and cocktails.

7. Bluestem

Bluestem is a fine dining restaurant that serves contemporary American cuisine. The menu includes creative dishes like foie gras torchon and duck breast with sweet potato gnocchi. The restaurant also has an extensive wine list.

8. Char Bar

Char Bar is another great spot for barbecue. This restaurant has a laid-back atmosphere and serves everything from burnt ends to smoked chicken to barbecue nachos. They also have a great selection of craft beer.

9. Happy Gillis

If you're looking for a casual breakfast or lunch spot, check out Happy Gillis. This restaurant serves sandwiches, salads, and other comfort food dishes. They also have a great selection of coffee and pastries.

10. The Antler Room

The Antler Room is a contemporary American restaurant that serves creative and flavorful dishes. The menu changes frequently, but you can always expect fresh and seasonal ingredients. The restaurant also has a great selection of wine and cocktails.

Conclusion

Whether you're in the mood for barbecue, Mexican food, or fine dining, Kansas City has something for everyone. These 10 restaurants are just a few of the many great places to eat in the city. So next time you're hungry, head to one of these spots and enjoy a delicious meal.
