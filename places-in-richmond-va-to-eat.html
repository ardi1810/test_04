Places In Richmond, Va To Eat

Introduction

Richmond, VA is a city renowned for its lively food scene. With a wide range of restaurants, cafes, and bars, there's always something new to try. Whether you're a local or just visiting, here are some of the best places to eat in Richmond.

1. The Roosevelt

Located in the heart of Church Hill, The Roosevelt is a popular spot for foodies. The restaurant offers a farm-to-table menu with dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. Their menu changes seasonally, so there's always something new to try.

2. L'Opossum

This French-inspired restaurant is a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique dining experience. The menu features dishes like escargot, foie gras, and beef tartare. The decor is also worth noting, with a quirky and colorful interior that's sure to make your meal memorable.

3. ZZQ

If you're in the mood for barbecue, ZZQ is the place to go. This restaurant has won numerous awards for their smoked meats, including brisket, ribs, and pulled pork. They also offer a variety of sides, like mac and cheese and smoked brisket beans.

4. Perly's

Perly's is a classic Jewish deli that's been a Richmond institution for decades. The menu features classic deli favorites like matzo ball soup, pastrami sandwiches, and potato latkes. They also have a great selection of bagels and lox.

5. Mama J's

This soul food restaurant serves up comfort food favorites like fried chicken, mac and cheese, and collard greens. The portions are generous, and the prices are reasonable. It's a great spot for a casual meal with friends or family.

6. The Daily Kitchen & Bar

If you're looking for healthy options, The Daily Kitchen & Bar has you covered. They offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes, as well as meat and seafood options. The menu is diverse and flavorful, with dishes like sweet potato tacos and lentil burgers.

7. Kuba Kuba

This Cuban restaurant is a local favorite for its authentic cuisine and lively atmosphere. The menu features dishes like ropa vieja, empanadas, and Cuban sandwiches. They also have a great selection of mojitos and other cocktails.

8. Comfort

As the name suggests, Comfort is all about comfort food. The menu features Southern classics like fried chicken, biscuits and gravy, and shrimp and grits. They also have a great selection of craft beer and cocktails.

9. The Boathouse

Located on the James River, The Boathouse offers stunning views and delicious seafood. The menu features dishes like crab cakes, shrimp and grits, and lobster mac and cheese. They also have a great selection of wines and cocktails.

10. The Stables at Belmont

This farm-to-table restaurant is located on the grounds of the historic Belmont estate. The menu features dishes made from ingredients grown on the estate, as well as locally sourced meats and produce. The atmosphere is elegant and rustic, making it a great spot for a special occasion.

Conclusion

Whether you're in the mood for barbecue, soul food, or French cuisine, Richmond has something to offer. These ten restaurants are just a sampling of the city's diverse food scene. So the next time you're in town, be sure to check them out!
