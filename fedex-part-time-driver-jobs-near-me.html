Introduction

Are you looking for a part-time job that you can do on your own schedule? Look no further than FedEx! FedEx is one of the largest courier companies in the world and they are always in need of part-time drivers to help deliver packages. In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about FedEx part-time driver jobs near you in 2023.

What Are Part-Time Driver Jobs at FedEx?

FedEx part-time driver jobs involve driving delivery trucks to various locations to drop off packages. These jobs are part-time and offer flexible schedules, making them ideal for students or anyone looking for a side gig. Part-time drivers are responsible for safely and efficiently delivering packages to customers while providing excellent customer service.

Requirements for Part-Time Driver Jobs at FedEx

To become a part-time driver at FedEx, there are a few requirements you must meet. First, you must be at least 21 years old and have a valid driver's license. You must also be able to lift and move packages that weigh up to 75 pounds. Additionally, you must pass a background check and drug test.

How Much Do Part-Time Drivers at FedEx Make?

The pay for part-time drivers at FedEx varies depending on location, experience, and the number of hours worked. The average hourly rate for a part-time driver at FedEx is $18. However, this rate can range from $15 to $25 per hour.

Benefits of Working as a Part-Time Driver at FedEx

There are several benefits to working as a part-time driver at FedEx. First, you get to work on your own schedule. This means you can choose to work as many or as few hours as you want. Additionally, part-time drivers are eligible for benefits such as healthcare, retirement plans, and tuition assistance.

How to Apply for Part-Time Driver Jobs at FedEx

To apply for a part-time driver job at FedEx, visit their website and search for available positions in your area. You will need to create a profile and submit your resume and other relevant information. If you meet the requirements and pass the background check and drug test, you will be contacted for an interview.

Tips for Getting Hired as a Part-Time Driver at FedEx

To increase your chances of getting hired as a part-time driver at FedEx, make sure your resume is up to date and highlights your relevant experience. Additionally, be prepared to answer questions about your driving record and customer service skills during the interview. Dress professionally and be on time for your interview to make a good impression.

Training for Part-Time Drivers at FedEx

Once you are hired as a part-time driver at FedEx, you will receive on-the-job training to learn how to safely and efficiently operate a delivery truck. You will also learn about FedEx's policies and procedures regarding package delivery and customer service.

Conclusion

If you are looking for a flexible part-time job with great benefits and competitive pay, consider becoming a part-time driver at FedEx. With their extensive network and growing demand for package delivery, there are always opportunities available. Apply today to start your career as a part-time driver at FedEx!
