Types of RN Jobs in De Queen AR

There are several types of RN jobs available in De Queen, AR. These include:

2. Home Health RN

Home health RNs provide medical care to patients in their homes. They may administer medication, monitor vital signs, and provide wound care. They also work closely with patients’ families to ensure that they are receiving the best possible care.

3. Long-Term Care RN

Long-term care RNs work in nursing homes and other long-term care facilities. They are responsible for providing medical care to patients with chronic illnesses or disabilities. They may also provide basic daily care, such as helping patients with bathing and dressing.
