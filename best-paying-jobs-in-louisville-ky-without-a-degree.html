Introduction

Louisville, Kentucky is a city that is home to many job opportunities. However, not everyone has a college degree, and finding a high-paying job without one can be challenging. In this article, we will explore the top-paying jobs in Louisville, KY that do not require a degree in 2023.

1. Air Traffic Controller

Air traffic controllers are responsible for directing and monitoring the movement of airplanes in and out of the airport. This job requires a lot of training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for an air traffic controller in Louisville, KY, is $122,000 per year.

2. Commercial Pilot

If you have a passion for flying, you can become a commercial pilot in Louisville, KY. This job requires a lot of training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for a commercial pilot in Louisville, KY, is $105,000 per year.

3. Elevator Installer and Repairer

Elevator installers and repairers are responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing elevators. This job requires a lot of training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for an elevator installer and repairer in Louisville, KY, is $95,000 per year.

4. Real Estate Broker

Real estate brokers help people buy and sell properties. This job does not require a college degree, but it requires a license. The average salary for a real estate broker in Louisville, KY, is $85,000 per year.

5. Dental Hygienist

Dental hygienists help dentists clean teeth, take x-rays, and educate patients on oral health. This job requires an associate's degree and a license, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for a dental hygienist in Louisville, KY, is $75,000 per year.

6. Web Developer

Web developers design and create websites. This job does not require a college degree, but it requires a lot of training and experience. The average salary for a web developer in Louisville, KY, is $70,000 per year.

7. Electrician

Electricians install and maintain electrical systems. This job requires training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for an electrician in Louisville, KY, is $65,000 per year.

8. Plumber

Plumbers install and repair plumbing systems. This job requires training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for a plumber in Louisville, KY, is $60,000 per year.

9. HVAC Technician

HVAC technicians install and maintain heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. This job requires training and certification, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for an HVAC technician in Louisville, KY, is $55,000 per year.

10. Paralegal

Paralegals assist lawyers in preparing legal documents, researching, and organizing cases. This job requires an associate's degree, but it does not require a college degree. The average salary for a paralegal in Louisville, KY, is $50,000 per year.

There are many high-paying jobs in Louisville, KY, that do not require a college degree. If you are passionate about one of these careers, you can start your training and certification today and start earning a good salary in 2023.
