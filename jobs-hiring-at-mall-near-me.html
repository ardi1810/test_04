The State of Jobs at Malls in 2023

Malls used to be the go-to destination for shopping and entertainment, but in recent years, they have faced declining foot traffic and sales. This has led to closures of many stores and even entire malls. However, the good news is that some malls are still thriving and are actively looking to hire new employees.

Why Work at a Mall?

Working at a mall can be a great opportunity for those looking for a flexible schedule or a part-time job. Many mall jobs also offer employee discounts and opportunities for advancement. Plus, with the variety of stores and businesses within a mall, there are a wide range of job opportunities available.

What Types of Jobs are Available at Malls?

There are a variety of jobs available at malls, including:
  • Retail Sales Associate
  • Cashier
  • Food Service Worker
  • Custodian
  • Security Guard
  • Marketing Coordinator

How to Find Jobs Hiring at Malls Near You

There are a few ways to find job openings at malls in your area. One option is to visit the mall's website and look for a "Careers" or "Jobs" section. Another option is to visit the stores within the mall and inquire about job openings. You can also check popular job search websites like Indeed or Monster for listings.

What to Expect During the Hiring Process

The hiring process for mall jobs can vary depending on the store or business. Generally, you can expect to fill out an application and potentially participate in a group interview. Some stores may also require a background check or drug test.

Tips for Landing a Job at a Mall

Here are a few tips to increase your chances of landing a job at a mall:
  • Be prepared to work weekends and holidays, as these are often the busiest times for malls.
  • Dress appropriately for the job you are applying for.
  • Be friendly and personable during the interview process.
  • Highlight any relevant experience you may have, such as customer service or sales experience.

The Benefits of Working at a Mall

In addition to the employee discounts and opportunities for advancement mentioned earlier, working at a mall can also provide valuable job skills such as customer service, sales, and time management. Plus, with the variety of businesses within a mall, there is always the potential to network and make valuable connections.

Conclusion

While malls may not be as popular as they once were, they still offer a variety of job opportunities for those looking for part-time or flexible work. By following the tips outlined in this article, you can increase your chances of landing a job at a mall near you.
