Introduction

Kinston, North Carolina is a small town that is known for its delicious food. In this article, we will explore some of the best places to eat in Kinston. Whether you are a local or a visitor, you won't be disappointed with the culinary offerings in this town.

The Chef and the Farmer

The Chef and the Farmer is a must-visit restaurant in Kinston. This restaurant is owned by Vivian Howard, a well-known chef and TV personality. The menu features Southern cuisine with a modern twist, and the restaurant is known for its farm-to-table concept. The Chef and the Farmer is the perfect place to go for a special occasion or a romantic dinner.

The Boiler Room

If you are looking for a more casual dining experience, check out The Boiler Room. This restaurant is located in the basement of the Kinston's Mother Earth Brewing Company. The menu features classic pub fare such as burgers, wings, and fries. You can also enjoy a wide selection of craft beers and cocktails.

King's Restaurant

King's Restaurant is a Kinston institution that has been around for over 75 years. This family-owned restaurant is famous for its delicious fried chicken, barbecue, and seafood. The portions are generous, and the prices are reasonable. King's Restaurant is the perfect place to go for a comforting and satisfying meal.

The Red Room

The Red Room is a trendy restaurant and bar that serves up creative cocktails and small plates. The menu features a variety of international flavors and ingredients. The Red Room is a great place to go for a night out with friends or a date night.

The Kinstonian

The Kinstonian is a cozy cafe that serves breakfast and lunch. The menu features classic American dishes such as pancakes, omelets, and sandwiches. The prices are affordable, and the portions are generous. The Kinstonian is a great place to go for a casual meal with family or friends.

Oliver's

Oliver's is a fine dining restaurant that is located in the historic Grainger Hill district. The menu features upscale cuisine with a Southern twist. The restaurant is known for its excellent service and elegant atmosphere. Oliver's is the perfect place to go for a special occasion or a romantic dinner.

The Farmer's Market

The Kinston Farmer's Market is a great place to go for fresh, local produce and other food items. The market is open on Saturdays from 8 am to 1 pm. You can find everything from fruits and vegetables to baked goods and artisanal cheeses. The Farmer's Market is a great place to go for a healthy and delicious meal.

Queen Street Deli

Queen Street Deli is a charming sandwich shop that is located in downtown Kinston. The menu features a variety of sandwiches, salads, and soups. The ingredients are fresh, and the portions are generous. Queen Street Deli is a great place to go for a quick and satisfying lunch.

Bojangles

Bojangles is a fast-food chain that is known for its delicious fried chicken and biscuits. The Kinston location is conveniently located off Highway 70. Bojangles is a great place to go for a quick and affordable meal.

The Chef's Table

The Chef's Table is a unique dining experience that is located inside the Kinston Farmer's Market. The menu features a rotating selection of dishes that are created using ingredients from the market. The restaurant only seats 24 people, so reservations are recommended. The Chef's Table is a great place to go for a one-of-a-kind meal.

Conclusion

Kinston, North Carolina is a foodie's paradise. Whether you are in the mood for Southern comfort food or international cuisine, you can find it in Kinston. These 10 places to eat are just the tip of the iceberg. There are many more great restaurants and cafes to explore in this charming town.
