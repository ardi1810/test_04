Best Places To Eat In Charlotte, Nc In 2023

Introduction

Charlotte, North Carolina, is known for its Southern hospitality and delicious food. From upscale restaurants to casual eateries, there is no shortage of options for foodies in the Queen City. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best places to eat in Charlotte in 2023.

1. Kindred

Kindred, located in the Davidson area, is a must-visit restaurant for food lovers. The menu features an array of creative dishes, including their famous milk bread and roasted chicken. The restaurant has a cozy, intimate atmosphere that adds to the dining experience.

2. Haberdish

If you’re a fan of Southern-style cuisine, Haberdish is the perfect spot for you. This restaurant in NoDa serves up delicious fried chicken, biscuits, and craft cocktails. The decor is rustic and charming, with exposed brick walls and vintage light fixtures.

3. The Stanley

The Stanley, located in the Elizabeth neighborhood, is a popular spot for brunch and dinner. The restaurant’s menu features seasonal and locally sourced ingredients, with dishes like shrimp and grits and roasted pork belly. The atmosphere is trendy yet cozy, with an open kitchen and modern decor.

4. Soul Gastrolounge

Soul Gastrolounge is a hip and trendy spot in Plaza Midwood that serves up creative small plates and cocktails. The menu features dishes like Korean BBQ pork belly tacos and buffalo cauliflower bites. The restaurant has a lively atmosphere, with a rooftop patio and a DJ on weekends.

5. 204 North Kitchen & Cocktails

204 North Kitchen & Cocktails is a farm-to-table restaurant in Uptown Charlotte. The menu features dishes made with locally sourced ingredients, like the Asheville mushroom risotto and the Carolina shrimp and grits. The restaurant has a sophisticated yet relaxed atmosphere, with a patio and a bar area.

6. Bardo

Bardo, located in the South End neighborhood, is a contemporary American restaurant with an emphasis on seasonal ingredients. The menu features dishes like lamb belly and duck breast, as well as a variety of small plates. The restaurant has a minimalist, modern decor and an open kitchen.

7. Yafo Kitchen

Yafo Kitchen is a fast-casual Mediterranean restaurant with locations in Uptown Charlotte and SouthPark. The menu features build-your-own bowls, salads, and pitas, with options like falafel, chicken shawarma, and hummus. The restaurant has a bright and colorful decor and a friendly atmosphere.

8. The Fig Tree Restaurant

The Fig Tree Restaurant, located in the historic Elizabeth neighborhood, is a fine-dining establishment that specializes in French and Italian cuisine. The menu features dishes like escargot and risotto, as well as an extensive wine list. The restaurant has an elegant and romantic atmosphere, with candlelit tables and a fireplace.

9. Vana

Vana is a Japanese-inspired restaurant in South End that serves up sushi, ramen, and small plates. The menu features dishes like the spicy tuna roll and the miso ramen, as well as a variety of sake and cocktails. The restaurant has a sleek and modern decor, with an open kitchen and a sushi bar.

10. Haberdish

If you’re in the mood for dessert, head over to Amélie’s French Bakery in NoDa. This charming bakery serves up an array of pastries, cakes, and macarons, as well as coffee and tea. The decor is whimsical and cozy, with plush armchairs and vintage chandeliers.

Conclusion

Charlotte, NC is a foodie’s paradise, with a variety of restaurants and eateries to suit every palate. Whether you’re in the mood for Southern-style comfort food or contemporary American cuisine, there’s a restaurant in Charlotte that will satisfy your cravings. We hope this article has given you some inspiration for your next dining adventure in the Queen City.
